Operational and governance risks that must be addressed before full enterprise rollout.

🔴
Low Attendance & Completion Rates
Only 7–8 of 28 expected participants attended live sessions. Just 4 participants completed all Moodle activities — a 14% completion rate against target. If not corrected, this pattern will scale to 1,000+ users, severely limiting ROI.
HIGH
