Executive Signals
📌
AALA dominates enrollment with 278 students (27.1%). Liberal Arts remains the institution's largest academic home, creating a concentration dependency that warrants strategic monitoring for program-level risks.
🌏
97.9% of students are Marshallese citizens, reflecting a highly homogeneous population. The institution serves as the primary higher education gateway for this Pacific Island nation.
🔄
Returnees and readmits represent 90.7% of enrollment — only 95 true first-timers this term. This signals strong institutional dependency on re-enrolled students rather than a robust new-student pipeline.
⚠️
134 students (13.1%) ended the term with "Inactive" standing — a significant attrition signal. Another 301 students show "To Be Determined," indicating incomplete grade processing or unresolved standing.
🎯
BAEE leads academic performance among large programs with avg GPA of 2.90. DUAL program tops all at 3.49 but has only 14 students. CCCT-W and AALA-BM show the lowest performance (below 1.5 avg GPA).
👩‍🎓
Female students outnumber males 57% to 43% (581 vs. 443). This gender composition is consistent with Pacific Island college enrollment trends and may inform targeted support programming.
📊
145 students (14.2%) have a 0.00 GPA, suggesting new enrollment, incomplete grades, or inactive academic records. These students represent a priority group for early advising and intervention.
⚡
Full-time rate stands at 62.5% (640 students). With 37.5% part-time, the institution should evaluate financial aid implications, FTE-based funding formulas, and completion rate impacts by enrollment intensity.

Analytical Assumptions & Governance Notes

  • Dashboard treats this file as a term-level enrollment census. Each row represents one unique enrolled student.
  • No duplicate SONIS_IDs were detected — dataset is clean for headcount reporting.
  • GPA averages exclude records with CUM_GPA = 0.00 to avoid skewing academic performance metrics. These 145 students are flagged separately.
  • Credit completion ratio may exceed 100% where cumulative credits earned exceed attempted, often due to transfer credit or system entry methodology. This is noted as a data characteristic, not an error.
  • No withdrawal data is present in the dataset this term (WITHDRAWN = 0 for all students).
  • Developmental education fields (HAS_DEVED_ENGLISH, HAS_DEVED_MATH) were not included in the pre-aggregated file — these sections reflect data limitations from the source report.
  • Placement fields (INITIAL_ENGLISH_PLACEMENT, INITIAL_MATH_PLACEMENT, etc.) were not available in the pre-aggregated export — excluded from readiness analysis.
  • No personally identifiable information (names, SSN, email) is displayed in this dashboard in accordance with FERPA and institutional data governance policy.
  • All filters are applied client-side to pre-computed aggregates. Totals remain as per the official enrollment census.
