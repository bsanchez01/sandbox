📖 Data Dictionary

Overall Score Weighted % of total possible points earned to date (from MindTap gradebook).
Total Points Raw cumulative points accumulated across all completed assignments.
Module Score Arithmetic mean of all scored (non-null) assignments within a module.
Completion Rate % of the 109 graded assignments that have a recorded score (non-blank).
At-Risk Student whose Overall Score falls below the user-selected threshold (default 75%).
Score Band Categorical bucket: 90–100 (A), 80–89 (B), 70–79 (C), 60–69 (D), <60 (F).
Class Avg 87.97% — pulled directly from the MindTap export aggregate row.
Missing Any assignment with a blank/null cell in the student's row (not yet submitted or graded).
  • Blank cells = Not completed. MindTap leaves cells blank for unsubmitted or ungraded work. These are counted as missing — not as zero.
  • Module 10 & 20 absent. The export contains Modules 01–09, 11–19, and 21. Modules 10 and 20 were not exported (likely not yet assigned or omitted by instructor).
  • Student IDs not populated. The "Student ID" column is blank in the export; the User ID column is used as a unique identifier.
  • Class average row (row 7 of file, Total Points=9149, Score=87.97%) treated as cohort-level aggregate, not a third student.
  • Scores are percentages (0–100%). Values like "61.54%" are preserved as-is from MindTap (some assignments are scored on non-integer scales).
